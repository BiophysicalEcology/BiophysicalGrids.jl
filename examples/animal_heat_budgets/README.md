# Animal heat budget examples

Placeholder for examples that drive animal/plant heat budget models with
microclimate outputs from `simulate_microclimate`.

Planned examples:
- Ectotherm body temperature from operative temperature and thermoregulation model
- Endotherm heat balance (metabolic rate, evaporative water loss)
- Grid-based activity and survival predictions

These will use the `MicroResult` returned by `simulate_microclimate` as forcing
for heat budget calculations.
